# StreakFlow

**Live:** [https://shadmansakibrahman.github.io/StreakFlow](https://shadmansakibrahman.github.io/StreakFlow)

Build better habits, one day at a time. StreakFlow is a clean, modern habit tracker that helps you stay consistent with streak tracking, analytics, and cross-device sync.

## Features

- Create habits with emoji icons, colors, and flexible schedules (daily, weekly, or custom days)
- Track daily by tapping the circle next to each habit to mark it complete
- Build streaks by completing habits on consecutive days
- View analytics with completion charts, streak leaderboards, and activity heatmaps
- Search, filter & sort your habits by name, date, or streak
- Archive & restore habits you want to pause without losing data
- Switch themes between light, dark, or system mode
- Sync across devices — sign in anywhere and your data is already there
- Export your data as a JSON backup anytime
- Install as an app — works as a PWA on mobile and desktop

## How to Use

1. **Sign up** — Create an account with email/password or sign in with Google
2. **Create habits** — Tap "New Habit", pick a name, emoji, color, and schedule
3. **Track daily** — On the dashboard, tap the circle next to each habit to mark it done
4. **Build streaks** — Stay consistent on your due dates to grow your streak
5. **Check analytics** — Head to the Analytics page for charts, completion rates, and your streak leaderboard
6. **Sync everywhere** — Sign in on any device and everything is already there
